Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. Kohli's cricket journey began with the West Delhi Cricket Academy and he quickly rose through the ranks, making his debut for Delhi in the Ranji Trophy at the age of 15. He led the Indian Under-19 team to victory in the 2008 World Cup and made his international debut later that year.
